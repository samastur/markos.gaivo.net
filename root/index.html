About me

After an embarrassing start writing backup software, I have been working in technology for almost 20 years in a variety of roles before settling down as a full-stack web developer with a penchant for front-end development.

I now work as an independent software consultant specializing in web solutions for low-resource environments which is another way of saying I use web technologies to build websites and mobile applications that work well everywhere.

You can read more about what I do (and did) at LinkedIn or contact me for further information.

I live in Ljubljana, Slovenia, have a wonderful wife, hate making phone calls and don't have a pet. In my spare time I like to read books, travel, ski and design social software for introverts.
